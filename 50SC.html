Babai's Secret Punugulu Recipe

Punugulu is a common street food in the Telugu states. It is a deep fried snack made with rice, urad dal and other spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Idli Batter
  • 1 tablespoon Semolina
  • 1/4 cup chopped Oninon
  • curry leaves
  • finely chopped ginger
  • 2 chopped green chillies
  • 2 teaspoon coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • oil to deep fry

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the batter, suji, onions, curry leaves, ginger, chillies, corriander and cumin seeds.
  2. Heat oil for deep frying in a kadai.
  3. Add spoonfuls of batter in the oil
  4. Keep turning the punugulu over until both sides are crisp and golden brown.
  5. Once done, then remove the punugulu with a slotted spoon and drain the excess oil.
  6. Serve Punugulu hot by pairing them with peanut chutney.
